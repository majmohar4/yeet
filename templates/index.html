🔐 Privacy & data policy
What we collect
  • Files, folders, and clipboard items you upload — stored on our servers
  • IP addresses (rate limiting & abuse prevention)
  • Upload timestamps and file metadata
  • Browser session ID stored in localStorage (for delete/pin permissions)
What we don't collect
  • No accounts, email addresses, or names required
  • No browsing history or cross-site tracking
  • No third-party analytics or ad trackers
  • No file content analysis (virus scanning only)
Security
  • All connections encrypted via HTTPS
  • Virus scanning on every upload (ClamAV)
  • Rate limiting + per-IP daily upload bandwidth cap
  • Password hashing with bcrypt (we never see your passwords)
  • HTML/SVG/JS uploads can never render or execute through this site — they are served as source text. Executables are download-only.
  • 🔥 Burn-after-read files are wiped from disk the instant they are served
Data retention
  • Files expire after your chosen time (1h – 7d)
  • Expired files archived for 24h (server access only), then permanently deleted
  • Burn-after-read files skip the archive — gone immediately on first download
  • Audit logs retained for 90 days for abuse investigation
  • IP-based rate limit entries deleted after 2 hours
Public clipboard ⚠
  • The 📋 Public clipboard panel is global — every visitor sees every non-expired paste
  • Don't put secrets, credentials, or personal data in there
  • Only you can pin or delete the items you posted (verified via your browser session)
Your rights
  • Delete your own files and clipboard items anytime from the session you uploaded them
  • Everything auto-deletes after expiry — no residual copies
  • No account needed; nothing tied to your identity
Abuse & DMCA

We do not tolerate illegal, harmful, or copyrighted content uploaded without permission. Files will be removed immediately upon valid report.
