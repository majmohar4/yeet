🔐 Privacy & data policy
What we collect
  • Files you upload (stored on our servers)
  • IP addresses (rate limiting & abuse prevention)
  • Upload timestamps and file metadata
  • Browser session ID stored in localStorage (for delete permissions)
What we don't collect
  • No accounts, email addresses, or names required
  • No browsing history or cross-site tracking
  • No third-party analytics or ad trackers
  • No file content analysis (virus scanning only)
Security
  • All connections encrypted via HTTPS
  • Virus scanning on every upload (ClamAV)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Password hashing with bcrypt (we never see your passwords)
Data retention
  • Files expire after your chosen time (1h – 7d)
  • Expired files archived for 24h (server access only), then permanently deleted
  • Audit logs retained for 90 days for abuse investigation
  • IP-based rate limit entries deleted after 2 hours
Your rights
  • Delete your own files anytime from the session you uploaded them
  • All files automatically deleted after expiry — no residual copies
  • No account needed; nothing tied to your identity
Abuse & DMCA

We do not tolerate illegal, harmful, or copyrighted content uploaded without permission. Files will be removed immediately upon valid report.
